Dan Bing

Description

My favorite breakfast in all of Taiwan.

Ingredients

  • Flour of some kind
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Try Sauce
  1. Mix the flour and eggs.
  2. Turn them into a crepe/pancake type thing.
  3. Apply vegetables and cheese liberally.
  4. Roll it up like a little burrito.
  5. Apply Try Sauce on top.
