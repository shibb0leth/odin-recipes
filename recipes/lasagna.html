Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • More cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano
  1. Put some tomato sauce in a dish.
  2. Put the noodles in the dish.
  3. Cover the top in as much cheese as you can.
  4. Bake in the most fiery hell.
  5. Put a little oregano on top for style.
